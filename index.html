Why Choose Type II Deionized Water?

ASTM Type II deionized water is often referred to as the "workhorse" of the modern laboratory. It provides a level of purity that is essential for a wide range of analytical and clinical applications where the presence of ions or organic contaminants could compromise results.

Unlike standard distilled water, which may still contain trace minerals and volatile organics, our certified Type II DI water undergoes a rigorous purification process including carbon filtration, reverse osmosis, and mixed-bed deionization to achieve a resistivity of >1.0 MΩ·cm.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this water suitable for CPAP machines?

While CPAP machines typically require distilled water to prevent scale buildup, ASTM Type II DI water is even purer and is perfectly safe for use in humidification systems.

How long can I store DI water?

Once opened, DI water will slowly absorb carbon dioxide from the air, which can lower its resistivity. For critical lab work, we recommend using the water within 30 days of opening.

What is the difference between Type I and Type II?

Type I water is "Ultrapure" (18.2 MΩ·cm) and is used for ultra-sensitive analytical work like HPLC. Type II is suitable for most general laboratory and industrial tasks.
